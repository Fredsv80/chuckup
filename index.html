❓ FAQ

Is my data safe?
Your API keys and analyses stay in your own browser (localStorage). Nothing is sent to any server — the app is just an HTML file.
Will I see updates automatically?
Yes. The app is served from a shared link. When it's updated, you'll get the new version automatically the next time you open the link.
If I clear my browser, do I lose everything?
Yes — your credentials and history are stored locally. API keys are easy to re-enter, but your analysis history would be lost.
Can I use it offline?
No. An internet connection is required to call AI providers and push platforms. The app itself loads from the shared link.
